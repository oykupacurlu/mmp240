Meredith Grey and the team of doctors at Grey Sloan Memorial who are faced with life-or-death decisions daily. They seek comfort from one another, and, at times, more than just friendship.

Episodes

1. A Hard Day's Night

While enrolled in a demanding residency program, Meredith Grey and four other first-year surgical interns try to balance life and work at Seattle Grace Hospital.

2. The First Cut is the Deepest

A young woman clings to life after a brutal attack, leaving the surgical staff optimistic; Meredith puts her career on the line to save a newborn in the hospital nursery.

3.Winning a Battle, Losing the War

The interns compete to treat severe injuries as patients from the annual bike-messenger race fill the emergency room. Christina and Izzie face a difficult situation with a brain dead patient.

4. No Man's Land

Cristina treats a former Seattle Grace scrub nurse and is forever changed. Izzie's past comes back to haunt her and makes a case difficult.

5. Shake Your Groove Thing

Meredith believes she may have erred during surgery and endangered her career; nothing goes as planned for Izzie, who tries to throw a party for her new boyfriend.

6. If Tomorrow Never Comes

Alex works hard to gain the trust of Annie, a shy patient with a gargantuan tumor, only to make a fool of himself in front of her later.
